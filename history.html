History

The first Vocaloids were the English voicebanks Leon and Lola, the software's popularity soared with the introduction of Japanese VOCALOIDs and amazing character branding, most notably with Hatsune Miku in 2007.


In the following years, producers such as PinocchioP and Neru continued to release music.


Project: DIVA

On the 2nd of July of 2009, the highly anticipated rhythm game "Hatsune Miku: Project Diva" was released, allowing fans to hit buttons to the beat of their favourite Vocaloid songs.

Project: SEKAI

On the 30th of September of 2020, a more accessible rhythm game was released. This time the main focus was the world of the voice bank's world, hence the name "SEKAI", meaning "World".
