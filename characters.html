Popular Vocaloid Characters

Hatsune Miku

Hatsune Miku (初音ミク) is the most iconic vocaloid character ever, she is famous for her clear voice and is used in a lot of Vocaloid songs.

Megurine Luka

Megurine Luka (巡音ルカ), famous for her long pink hair and mature voice.

KAITO

Classic male Vocaloid with a warm voice, often used for ballads.

GUMI

Gumi (グミ), posseses an energetic voice, often used for rock or sentimental upbeat songs.
